Research and Publications

RACfoundation - Performance Study of Fuel Price Predicting Methods (Group Project) (2022)
Summary:
RACfoundation exhibited a problem of forecasting fuel prices using external component prices and data. Evaluated and investigated the Regression Model, Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average model (ARIMA), and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) Neural Network to forecast the Petrol Fuel Pump Price by using the dataset provided by RACfoundation.

Toshiba - Storing and Retrieving Robotic Cubes in a Dynamic Environment (Group Project) (2021)
Summary:
Toshiba presented a problem of storing and retrieving robotic cubes in a dynamic environment. We structured an optimal strategy to store and retrieve blocks considering maximising storage capacity and minimising retrieval time by treating the problem in 2D and by implementing A* and Dijkstra path searching algorithms.

Met Office - Comparison of two resolutions of Tropical Cyclones datasets over Bangladesh (Group Project) (2022)
Summary
We explored the Tropical Cyclones that hit Bangladesh between 1991-2019 by analysing the dataset, provided by Met Office, which contained 9 ensembles of 12 separate Tropical Cyclones. - Utilised statistical analysis, data visualisation and forecasting via Long Short-Term Memory Neural Network to investigate the differences between the two datasets to justify the additional storage and computational efficiency between the two resolution datasets.
